The Arcade Machine


This seemingly inconspicuous arcade cabinet is actually a project in the works for the IxT club! Club members are working to turn this dusty arcade cabinet into a prime gaming machine for all to enjoy within the GCU eSports Arena. The arcade cabinet will run off of a Raspberry Pi equipped with a RetroPie OS to manage its library of classic arcade games. There are plans to connect the arcade machine to a Twitch API that will stream the games played on screen to the internet! A camera will be mounted onto the machine that will catch video of players competing for bragging rights or just having some casual fun! We hope that we can host weekly tournaments for GCU students to participate in playing classic titles such as Street Fighter II, Tekken, and more!

Havoc Badge


The Havoc Badge is a new way to get involved with GCU's sporting events! The Havocs shine like stars as their sleek and sharp badges light up the stands! Syncing with real time events, these sleek and sharp emblems can change color and patterns to the music being played! When badges are put together, they can even display images and animations! When not used at GCU related events, these stylish badges still prove useful as they can display different colors/patterns using the button located at the front.
